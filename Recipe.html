What you need to make Pounded Yam and Egusi

    The following will be required to make Pounded Yam and Egusi
  • a tuber of yam
  • a bowl of Egusi leaves
  • a bottle of palm oil
  • a melon seeds
  • Phomo
  • meat
  • crayfish
  • salt

after you get all these Ingredents you are now ready to make the food!!!

How to make Pounded Yam and Egusi Soup

making pounde yam and Egusi soup is actually quite easy if you follow the directions below

  1. boil the yam until it soft and ready for pounding
  2. pound the yam with Mortar and pestle until it is soft and ready to eat
  3. ground the melon seeds using a grinder
  4. boil the meat and phomo until they are soft
  5. cut out and wash the melon leaves
  6. put the ground melon seeds in a pot of boiling water
  7. add the meat and phomo and all the other ingredents
  8. put the plam oil, and allow to cook for a while
  9. add the Egusi leaves and stir until properly done

Your Lovely Pounded Yam and Egusi soup is ready to serve!!!
